About Au5

(A-U-Five)

The unmistakable sound of Au5 - futuristic sound design infused with melodic soundscapes. Au5 transports you to a utopian world that's as vibrant as it is monumental, consistently striving to push the boundaries of possibility within electronic music. From the stargazers to the neck-breakers, Au5's music has something we can all immerse ourselves in.

Born and raised in New Jersey, Au5 (Austin Collins) has been a lifelong musician. Trained in classical piano since age 4 and studying bass guitar and music production since age 13, Austin is a well-rounded composer and music technologist unbounded by genre.

"If you're looking for trendy music, turn away. If you're looking to embark on a journey, come with me."
